Admin's Guide to Telkon


NOTICES

Admin can post NOTICES to communicate with the residents of the society.
POST FOR OWNERS will be delivered to only the owners of the society.
POST FOR TENANTS will be delivered to only the tenants of the society.
POST FOR BOTH will be delivered to both owners and tenants.

COMPLAINTS

The COMPLAINTS are sorted by date, latest first.
Admin can mark a complaint as ESCALATE, PROCESSING or RESOLVE.
Also, admin can REMOVE a complaint, if not required anymore.

CONTACTS

Admin can maintain a directory of important CONTACTS for the residents.
SERVICE CONTACTS contains contact of utility provider nearby your area.
RWA/ASSOCIATION CONTACTS contains contacts of RWA/Association members.
INTERCOM CONTACTS contains intercom contacts, if present. Admin can ADD, EDIT or REMOVE a contact.

MEMBERS

All the MEMBERS of the society can be accessed from here.
RED ROW signifies that the member is reported.
Clicking on member record will show the full details of the members and the complaints registered.
Incase, this member is reported by his flatmate as non-genuine, admin can either BLOCK, CLEAN or REMOVE the member.

ADMINS

All the ADMINS of the society can be access from here.
There are two types of admins -
SUPER ADMIN has full privileges - posting a notice, replying on a complaint, maintaining contacts, action on members.
ADMIN also has all privileges accept to REMOVE a member and to ADD an admin.
Only a superadmin can add another admin.

PROFILE

You can edit your PROFILE like name, phone number etc, from the drop down menu available at the top right, under your name.

SOCIETY INFO

You can edit your SOCIETY INFO like number of flats etc, from the drop down menu available at the top right, under your name.

ACCOUNT

You can manage your ACCOUNT here. The following 2 options are available -
REMOVE If you don't want to be an admin of your society, click REMOVE.
DELETE If you want to delete your Telkon account permanently, click DELETE.
