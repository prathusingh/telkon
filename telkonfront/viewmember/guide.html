Member's Guide to Telkon


FORUMS

A member can post FORUMS to discuss anything. There are two types forums -
PRIVATE FORUMS are visible to the members of your society only.
PUBLIC FORUMS are visible to the members of other societies' as well.
You can mark a forum as FAVORITE to distinguish it from others. You can comment(FOR, NEUTRAL or AGAINST) on a forum.
Also, you can express your feeling against a forum through the EMOTICONS provided :p
You can view all the forums posted by you in the SIDE NAVIGATION BAR under 'My Forums'

NOTICES

A member can save NOTICES for later reference.
Saved notices can be accessed through 'Saved Notices' tab present in SIDE NAVIGATION BAR

COMPLAINTS

A member can REGISTER COMPLAINTS, may request an ESCALATION, if necessary or can REMOVE it when resolved.

CLASSIFIEDS

A member can post CLASSIFIEDS which will be visible to every member of Telkon.
Posted classifieds can be accessed from 'My Classifieds' in SIDE NAVIGATION BAR You can save classifieds as well. Access them under 'Saved Classifieds' tab present in SIDE NAVIGATION BAR

CONTACTS

Member can access the important CONTACTS -
SERVICE CONTACTS contains contact of utility provider nearby your area.
AROUND ME CONTACTS contains other important contacts around you.
RWA/ASSOCIATION CONTACTS contains contacts of RWA/Association members.
INTERCOM CONTACTS contains intercom contacts, if present.

FAMILY MEMBERS

You can view the family members/flatmates using Telkon from 'Family Members' tab available in SIDE NAVIGATION BAR.
REPORT Please report a family member, if you think its not genuine.

SOCIETY MEMBERS

You can also view all the members of your society using Telkon from 'Society Members' tab available in SIDE NAVIGATION BAR.

PROFILE

You can edit your PROFILE like name, phone number etc, from the drop down menu available at the top right, under your name.

ACCOUNT

You can manage your ACCOUNT here. The following 2 options are available -
REMOVE If you are moving to another society and you want to change your society, go for it.
DELETE If you want to delete your Telkon account permanently, go for it.
