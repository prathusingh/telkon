Motivation

Telkon came into existence because of several reasons. Have a look at some of those.

Improper use of Social Networking

Today, in the presence of several social networking platforms and tools, we feel connected to eveyone.But we do not know the person living next door. It is really something to worry about and needs an initiative.

Absence of Group Management Tool

Although, many applications provide group management feature but, they fail to manage it in a user-friendly and efficient manner because groups and clubs were not among their core features.



No Online Complaint Management

Maintaining complaints in registers is becoming obsolete. With everyhting going online, there is a need to maintain the residents' complaints online.

Less interaction at Living Place

Due to our busy schedule, we have almost ignored our life at living place. Studies show that interaction at living place offers mental strength, peace and relief.



Difficulty in finding Service Providers

Its really annoying when you could not find contact number of service provider like electrician, plumber etc, nearby your residence, when you really need it. There is a need of a proper management of important contacts that can accessed at any time, easily.

Need for Intelligent Advertising

The audience of an advertisement determines its success rate. For eg, it does not make sense to advertise for selling a car in 'cannot afford car' or 'do not need a car' community.

Huge paper loss - Go Green

With notices, advertisements etc, within the society premises, using paper, we are not only disobeying Green Revolution, also we are not reaching to each and every person it is meant for. There is a very simple solution that solves both the purposes.
We can really contribute to the society by going online - Notices and Classifieds. This way, we can save a lot of paper. If we calculate the savings for one year, we will realize the advantage of taking such a step. So, lets be a part of it.
