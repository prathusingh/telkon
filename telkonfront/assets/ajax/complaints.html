Complaints - Manage online
Managing records in registers has become obsolete these days. Its really difficult to maintain data, search a record, making changes or correction in a record, tracking a record etc, with this traditional approach.
In todays world, almost everything has gone online like booking travel tickets, ordering food, calling cab, paying utilities etc. Theres gotta be a reason for this. And the reason is quite simple - better & efficient management and User-friendly.

Telkon offers Online Complaint Management System for managing the societies' complaints in a better way by showing the actions taken on a complaint in a timeline fashion. The complaints are accessible to the complainants as well. They can track the complaint online and raise request for escalation, if necessary.
