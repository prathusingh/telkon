Classifieds - Intelligent advertising
As seen in the last few decades, the Advertising Industry has grown like anything. Many companies are making good money by providing a platform to the advertisers. But, does it make sense advertising for a product among the community who cannot afford it or among the community who does not require it. This flaw gave rise to the concept of Intelligent Advertisement.

Telkon ensures that the classifieds posted here will be circulated among the community who could be highly potential customer. The audience of an advertisement determines its success or conversion rate.
