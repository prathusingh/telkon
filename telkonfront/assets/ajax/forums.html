Forums - Connect with others
Today, due to the presence to several social networking platforms, we are well connected to the people we care about. The advancements in technology has made it possible to contact a person residing in completely different part of the world anytime you want. It really deserves a big round of applause - We are connected.
What is the issue when it comes to our living place. We do not know the person living next door. Actually, this Virtual Web World has made us so occupied that we do not have time for social interaction at our living place. Studies show that such interaction is really helpful in easing our lives, it also offers mental strength and peace.

Telkon offers two types of forums to interact with the people around us -
PRIVATE FORUMS to interact with people within our society.
PUBLIC FORUMS to interact with people outside our society.
