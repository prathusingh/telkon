Online Payment & Voting
Currently, the modes of payment available for paying monthly/quraterly/annually maintenance charges are Cash, Cheque and Netbanking. With these modes, residents can not make use of their Credit Cards.

Telkon is working on providing their users a Payment Portal that will have other modes of payments like Credit Card, PayTM etc alongwith the options mentioned above.
Also, Telkon will come up a Polling or Voting solution for activities with in a society.
