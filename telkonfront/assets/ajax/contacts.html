Contacts - Nearby service providers
Isn't it annoying not to find a single contact of a service provider like electrician, plumber, carpenter, cook, maid etc at the time when we need it the most? Well, not any more.

Telkon maintains a list of important contacts of service providers in your locality. Also, the society admins have been provided with an interface to share RWA/Association and Intercom numbers with residents so that the residents can reach out to them easliy.
